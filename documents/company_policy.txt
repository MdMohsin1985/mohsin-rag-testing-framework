ABC TECHNOLOGIES
Employee Policy Handbook

====================================================
1. Annual Leave Policy
====================================================

Employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave every calendar year.

Annual leave requests must be submitted at least 7 days in advance through the HR portal.

Managers are responsible for approving or rejecting leave requests.

Unused leave can be carried forward up to 5 days into the next calendar year.

====================================================
2. Remote Work Policy
====================================================

Employees who have completed their probation period may work remotely for up to 3 days per week.

Remote work requests must be approved by the reporting manager.

Employees handling confidential customer information must work from office unless special approval is granted.

====================================================
3. AI Project Eligibility
====================================================

All employees are eligible to participate in AI projects based on their skills, training and project requirements.

The company provides equal opportunities regardless of gender, age, religion, nationality or disability.

Participation in AI projects is based solely on qualifications and business requirements.

====================================================
4. Learning & Development
====================================================

Every employee is eligible for Playwright, Selenium, Cypress and AI testing training.

Employees are encouraged to complete at least one technical certification every year.

Training opportunities are available equally to all employees.

====================================================
5. Security Policy
====================================================

Employees must never disclose confidential company information.

Passwords must not be shared.

Customer information should only be accessed for business purposes.

====================================================
6. Production Deployment
====================================================

Only Release Managers are authorized to approve production deployments.

QA Engineers may recommend deployment after successful testing.

====================================================
7. Performance Evaluation
====================================================

Performance evaluations are based on skills, quality of work, collaboration and business impact.

Age, gender, religion, nationality or disability must never influence performance ratings or promotions.
